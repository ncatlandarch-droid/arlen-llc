About Arlan

Built on craftsmanship, driven by passion, and named after the hardest-working chihuahua in the lighting business.

The Founder

Meet Dylan Thomas

Dylan brings a rare combination of construction knowledge, artistic vision, and client-first salesmanship to every project. With years at Champion Windows, he mastered the technical side — precision measurement, structural awareness, and installation excellence.

Eight years as a professional hairdresser sharpened his aesthetic eye and taught him that the best results come from truly listening to what clients want. He learned that brilliance is in the details — and that philosophy carries into every lighting design.

Now, with Arlan LLC, Dylan combines both worlds: the precision of construction with the artistry of design. Every property is a canvas, and every installation is a masterpiece.
