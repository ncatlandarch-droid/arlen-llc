How It Works

From Drone to Dream Home

Our exclusive three-step process uses cutting-edge drone technology and AI-powered 3D visualization to give you complete control over your lighting design.

01

Precision Drone Scan

Our FAA-licensed drone captures 200+ high-resolution photographs of your property from every angle. The process takes just 15 minutes and requires zero disruption to your day.

02

Interactive 3D Design

Within 24 hours, your home becomes a photorealistic 3D digital twin in our platform. Rotate, zoom, and explore your property — then add lighting to any surface with a click.

03

Flawless Installation

Approve your design, receive an instant quote, and we install exactly what you envisioned. What you see in 3D is what you get in real life — guaranteed.

The Technology

Powered by AVA Technology

Our 3D Design Experience is built on AVA — the Adaptive Visualization Assistant developed by Think! Design & Planning. The same AI platform used for professional landscape architecture and urban design.

Professional Drones

FAA Part 107 certified operators using commercial-grade drones with 4K cameras capture every architectural detail of your property.

3D Digital Twins

Photogrammetry software processes 200+ images into a photorealistic 3D model. Hosted on Cesium Ion’s globe platform — the same technology used by NASA.

AI-Powered Design

Google Gemini AI generates realistic lighting previews on your 3D model. Our mascot Arlen guides you through options with voice assistance.
